For this first graph we are showing the profitability of the store in each state with the darker blue states being more profitable and the lighter blue/orange states being less profitable. Feel free to interact with the graph and hover over the states.
For the second graph we are showing the profitability of California as it is one of the most profitable states according to our previous diagram. And we can see that profits have risen exponentially from 2018-2021.
For the third graph we are showing the distribution of profits among the categories of Furniture, Office Supplies, and Technology. The numbers show in each square are the dollar values with Office Supplies being the most profitable in terms of gross sales.
For the final graph we are showing the forecast of quantity of sales for Office Supplies. With Office Supplies being the most profitable we would like to see how quantity of sales of Office Supplies will increase from month to month for a year into the future.
